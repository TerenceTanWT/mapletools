Skills Help
Perform a battle statistics and note down the damage ratio % of each skills.

Enter the skill name, in-built skill IED (e.g. V-matrix 20% IED), and damage ratio % of the skill.

If multiple skills have the exact same in-built IED, you can combine the skill's damage ratio % and enter it once. Remaining ratio % will be treated as 0% in-built IED.

If the skill has multiple IED sources, use "+" to separate the IED % (e.g. 30+20+20).

To delete a skill, tap on the added skill in blue.
Calculation Help
IED Old / New - Before/After final skill IED after adding in-built skill IED with stat window IED.

IED Diff - Difference between after and before IED.

FD - Final Damage increase due to new stat.

FD Avg - Average Final Damage increase due to new stat, after multiplying FD+ and damage ratio.

Overall FD - Overall Final Damage increase by multiplying FD+ Avg of IED, Att, and Dmg.
